---
title: "Troubleshoot common Glowply issues and error messages"
description: "Step-by-step solutions for the most common Glowply issues, including site loading problems, domain errors, login issues, and more."
---

If something is not working as expected, the solutions below cover the most common issues Glowply users encounter. If your issue is not listed here or the steps do not resolve it, [contact the support team](/contact-support) for direct assistance.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My site is not loading after creation">
    New sites take up to 60 seconds to fully provision after they are created. If your site is not loading:

    1. Wait at least 60 seconds from the time of creation.
    2. Hard-refresh your browser (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac).
    3. Try opening your site URL in a new browser tab or a private/incognito window.

    If the site is still not loading after a few minutes, contact support via WhatsApp and include your site URL.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My custom domain is not working">
    Custom domain issues are almost always related to DNS configuration or propagation time.

    1. Log in to your Glowply dashboard and go to **Website** → **Domain** to review the exact DNS records you need to add.
    2. Log in to your domain registrar (where you purchased the domain) and verify that the DNS records match exactly what Glowply requires.
    3. DNS changes can take up to **48 hours** to propagate globally. If you only recently updated your DNS records, wait and try again later.
    4. Use a DNS lookup tool to check whether your domain is resolving to the correct address.

    If the domain is still not working after 48 hours, [contact support](/contact-support) with your domain name and a screenshot of your DNS settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I cannot log in to my account">
    1. Make sure you are entering the email address you used to register with Glowply — not a different one.
    2. Check that Caps Lock is not on when entering your password.
    3. If you have forgotten your password, go to [glowply.com/my-account/lost-password](https://glowply.com/my-account/lost-password) and follow the instructions to reset it.
    4. Check your spam or junk folder for the password reset email if it does not arrive within a few minutes.

    If you are still unable to log in after resetting your password, contact support for account recovery assistance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My editor changes are not saving">
    1. Check your internet connection — the editor requires a stable connection to save changes.
    2. Refresh the page and re-apply your changes. Unsaved changes are sometimes lost on refresh, so re-enter them and save again.
    3. Try a different browser or disable browser extensions that may be interfering with the editor.
    4. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reload the editor.

    If changes consistently fail to save, contact support and describe which part of the editor you are using.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Customers cannot complete a payment on my store">
    1. Go to your store's payment settings and verify that your payment gateway credentials (API keys or account details) are entered correctly.
    2. Make sure the payment gateway account you are using is active and approved by the provider.
    3. Test with a small transaction amount to confirm the gateway is functioning.
    4. Check that the payment gateway you configured supports the card types or payment methods your customers are using.

    If the issue persists, contact support with the name of your payment gateway and a description of the error your customers see.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Images are not uploading">
    Glowply accepts images in **JPG**, **PNG**, and **WebP** formats with a maximum file size of **5 MB** per image.

    1. Check the file format — files in HEIC, BMP, TIFF, or other formats must be converted before uploading.
    2. Check the file size — compress large images using a free tool before re-uploading.
    3. Try uploading from a different browser or device.

    If a correctly formatted, correctly sized image still fails to upload, contact support and share the file name and size.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My site looks different on mobile">
    Glowply sites are responsive, but some design choices can cause layouts to look different on smaller screens.

    1. In the editor, click the **mobile preview** icon to switch to the mobile view and see exactly how your site appears on phones.
    2. Adjust fonts, spacing, image sizes, or section layouts specifically for the mobile view.
    3. Save your changes and test on an actual mobile device after saving.

    <Tip>
      Always check the mobile view before publishing major design changes. Most of your visitors are likely browsing on mobile.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---
title: "Set up shipping zones, rates, and delivery options"
description: "Learn how to set up shipping zones, flat-rate and free shipping, and local pickup so customers see accurate delivery costs at checkout."
---

Setting up shipping tells Glowply how much to charge customers for delivery based on where they are and how much they order. You can create multiple shipping zones with different rates, offer free shipping above a minimum order value, or let customers pick up their order in person.

## How shipping works

When a customer reaches checkout, Glowply checks their delivery address against your shipping zones and displays the rates you have configured. If no zone matches their address, they will not be able to complete the order — so make sure you have covered all the regions you deliver to.

## Create a shipping zone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Shipping Settings">
    From your dashboard, go to **Store** → **Shipping**. You will see a list of your current shipping zones, which is empty when you are just starting out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new zone">
    Click **Add shipping zone**. Give the zone a name, such as "Cairo" or "All of Egypt", to identify it in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the regions this zone covers">
    Choose the countries, governorates, or cities that belong to this zone. You can select one or multiple regions. Customers with addresses in these regions will see the rates you set for this zone at checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a shipping rate">
    Click **Add rate** within the zone. Enter a **Rate name** (e.g., "Standard delivery"), set the **Price**, and choose whether to base the rate on order value or apply it as a flat fee.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a free shipping threshold (optional)">
    To offer free shipping above a certain order value, add a second rate with a **Price of 0** and set the **Minimum order value** to the amount that qualifies. For example, set the minimum to 500 EGP to give free shipping on orders of 500 EGP or more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the zone">
    Click **Save**. The zone is now active and its rates will appear at checkout for customers in the selected regions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Offering free shipping above a minimum order value is one of the most effective ways to increase your average order size. Choose a threshold slightly above your current average order value — for example, if most orders are around 300 EGP, set the free shipping minimum at 400 EGP. Many customers will add another item to qualify.
</Tip>

## Add a flat-rate shipping option

A flat rate charges every customer in a zone the same delivery fee, regardless of what they ordered. This is the simplest option and works well if your products have similar weight and size. When adding a rate, enter a fixed **Price** and leave the minimum and maximum order values blank.

## Offer local pickup

If customers can collect their order from your location, you can add a pickup option instead of a delivery rate. Go to **Store** → **Shipping**, click **Add shipping zone**, name it "Local pickup", and add a rate with a price of 0. Select the relevant region, or leave it open to all regions if you want any customer to be able to choose pickup at checkout.

## Edit or remove a shipping zone

To update a zone, go to **Store** → **Shipping**, click the zone name, make your changes, and save. To delete a zone, open it and click **Delete zone**. Removing a zone means customers in those regions will no longer see a shipping option at checkout.

---

Every time a customer completes a purchase on your store, a new order appears in your Glowply dashboard. You can track each order from the moment it is placed through to delivery, and keep customers informed by updating the order status at each stage.

## View your orders

Go to **Store** → **Orders** to see a list of all orders. Each row shows the order number, customer name, total amount, date, and current status. Click any order to open the full order detail page.

<Info>
  Glowply notifies you of new orders by email. You may also receive WhatsApp notifications depending on your account notification settings. Check your account settings to manage how you are notified.
</Info>

## Order statuses

Each order moves through a series of statuses as you process it:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Order received, payment not yet confirmed. |
| **Processing** | Payment confirmed, order is being prepared. |
| **Shipped** | Order has been handed to a delivery carrier. |
| **Completed** | Order delivered and fulfilled. |
| **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled before fulfilment. |

## Process an order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the order">
    Go to **Store** → **Orders** and click the order you want to process. Review the items ordered, quantities, and the customer's delivery address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm payment">
    Check that the payment status shows **Paid**. If the customer chose cash on delivery, you will confirm payment when you receive it in person.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare the items">
    Pick and pack the items listed in the order. If any item is out of stock, contact the customer before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the status to Processing">
    On the order detail page, open the **Status** dropdown and select **Processing**, then click **Save**. The customer receives an update notification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hand off to delivery and mark as Shipped">
    Once you have given the package to your delivery carrier, update the status to **Shipped**. You can also enter a tracking number if your carrier provides one — it will be displayed to the customer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as Completed">
    After you confirm the order has been delivered, set the status to **Completed**. This closes the order and moves it to your completed order history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancel an order

To cancel an order, open it and set the status to **Cancelled**, then click **Save**. Add a note in the **Internal notes** field to record the reason. Cancelling an order does not automatically issue a refund — you will need to process any refund through your payment gateway separately.

## View customer details

Each order includes the customer's name, email address, phone number, and delivery address. You can use this information to follow up with the customer directly if there is an issue with their order.

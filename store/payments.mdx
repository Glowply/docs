Before your customers can complete a purchase, you need to connect at least one payment method to your store. Glowply integrates with payment gateways popular in Egypt and the MENA region, as well as cash on delivery — so you can accept payments in the way that works best for your customers.

## Available payment methods

The payment gateways available to you are listed inside your dashboard under **Store** → **Payments**. The exact options depend on your region. Common types include:

## Connect a payment gateway

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payment Settings">
    From your dashboard, go to **Store** → **Payments**. You will see a list of available payment methods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a gateway">
    Click **Set up** next to the payment method you want to activate. Each gateway shows a brief description of what you need to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Paste in the API key, merchant ID, or other credentials provided by the payment gateway. These are available in your account on the gateway's own website or app. The exact fields differ by gateway — Glowply labels each field clearly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and enable">
    Click **Save**. Toggle the method to **Enabled** so it appears at checkout. You can enable multiple payment methods at the same time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enable cash on delivery

Cash on delivery does not require a third-party gateway. Go to **Store** → **Payments**, find **Cash on delivery**, and toggle it to **Enabled**. You can optionally add a fee for this option, which will be added to the order total at checkout.

## Test your payments before going live

<Note>
  Before sharing your store link with customers, place a test order yourself and complete the checkout process. Verify that the payment goes through, the order appears in your dashboard, and you receive the expected confirmation notification. Most payment gateways provide a sandbox or test mode — use it during setup, then switch to live mode when you are ready to accept real payments.
</Note>

## Manage multiple payment methods

You can enable, disable, or edit any payment method at any time from **Store** → **Payments**. Changes take effect immediately at checkout. If you disable all payment methods, customers will not be able to complete a purchase, so always keep at least one method active.

<Warning>
  Keep your payment gateway credentials confidential. Never share your API keys or merchant secrets with anyone. Do not store them in emails or chat messages. If you believe your credentials have been compromised, regenerate them immediately from within your gateway's dashboard and update them in Glowply.
</Warning>

title: "Add and manage products in your Glowply online store"
description: "Learn how to add products, set prices, upload images, organize by category, add variants, and publish your catalog on your Glowply storefront."
---

Your Glowply store comes with a built-in product catalog where you can list everything you sell — physical goods, made-to-order items, or packaged services. Once a product is published, customers can browse your full collection and buy directly from your site.

## Add your first product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Products section">
    From your dashboard, go to **Store** → **Products**. You will see your product list, which is empty when you are just starting out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add product">
    Select **Add product** in the top-right corner to open the product editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the product details">
    Enter the **Product name** and write a **Description** that tells customers what they are buying. A clear, specific description helps customers make a decision and can improve your search visibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the price">
    Enter the **Price** for the product. If you are running a sale, you can also set a **Compare-at price** — the original price will appear crossed out on your storefront.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload product images">
    Click **Add images** and upload one or more photos of your product. You can drag to reorder images; the first image appears as the main thumbnail in your catalog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a category (optional)">
    Choose an existing category from the **Category** dropdown, or type a new category name to create one. Categories let customers filter your catalog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add variants (optional)">
    If your product comes in different options — such as size or color — open the **Variants** section and add each option and its values. You can set a separate price or stock quantity per variant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish the product">
    Set the status to **Published** and click **Save**. The product is now live on your storefront.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Good product photos significantly increase sales. Use natural light, photograph your product against a clean background, and upload at least two angles. Square images (1:1 ratio) display best across all devices.
</Tip>

## Edit an existing product

Open **Store** → **Products**, find the product you want to update, and click its name. Make your changes in the product editor, then click **Save**. Updates go live immediately.

## Organize products by category

Categories appear as filter options on your storefront, making it easier for customers to find what they need. To manage your categories, go to **Store** → **Categories**. You can rename, reorder, or delete categories at any time. Deleting a category does not delete the products inside it — those products simply become uncategorized.

## Publish and unpublish products

To hide a product from your storefront without deleting it, open the product editor and set the status to **Draft**, then save. Draft products are only visible to you in the dashboard. To make a product visible again, change the status back to **Published**.

<Note>
  The number of products you can add depends on your plan. Check **Account** → **Subscription** to see your current limit and upgrade if needed.
</Note>

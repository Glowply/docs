description: "Answers to the most common questions about Glowply — from getting started and building your site to billing, domains, and support."
---

Find answers to the most common questions about Glowply below. If you cannot find what you are looking for, visit the [troubleshooting guide](/troubleshooting) or [contact the support team](/contact-support).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need technical experience to use Glowply?">
    No. Glowply is designed specifically for business owners with no technical background. You do not need to know how to code, design, or manage hosting. Enter your business name, and Glowply takes care of everything else automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to create my website?">
    Just seconds. Glowply's AI builds your fully-hosted website or online store automatically after you enter your business details — the entire process takes under 45 seconds. Your site is live and accessible to visitors from the moment it is created.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create an online store with Glowply?">
    Yes. Every Glowply account includes a full e-commerce store. You can add products, set prices, manage orders, configure a shopping cart, connect payment gateways, and set up shipping — all from the same dashboard.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Glowply include hosting and security?">
    Yes. All Glowply sites are hosted on fast, secure infrastructure with 99.9% uptime. SSL (HTTPS) is included automatically, and Glowply handles all security patches and platform updates at no extra cost — you never need to manage a server.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I connect a custom domain?">
    Yes. On the Glowply paid plan you can connect any domain you own (for example, yourbusiness.com). If you are on the free trial or prefer not to use a custom domain, every Glowply site comes with a free subdomain in the format `mybusiness.myglowply.com`. See the [custom domain guide](/website/domain) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit my site after it is created?">
    Yes. You can update your site's text, images, colors, and layout at any time using the drag-and-drop editor. Changes are applied instantly — there is no need to republish or contact support. See the [website editor guide](/website/editor) for more details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get support?">
    Glowply offers two support channels. For quick questions, contact the team on WhatsApp — it is the fastest way to get a response. For technical issues, you can submit a support ticket through the platform by going to **Help** → **Submit a Ticket**. You can also search this documentation for answers. See [Contact support](/contact-support) for all options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods can my customers use?">
    Glowply integrates with popular payment gateways so your customers can pay by credit or debit card and other local payment methods available in your region. Payment gateway credentials are entered in your store settings and take effect immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription at any time?">
    Yes. There are no contracts or lock-in periods. You can cancel your Glowply subscription at any time from your account settings. Your site remains active until the end of the current billing period. See [Manage your subscription](/account/subscription) for instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is included in the 14-day free trial?">
    The free trial gives you full access to all Glowply features for 14 days. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, your site is paused until you subscribe to the Glowply Plan (699 EGP/month). Your content and settings are preserved and restored when you subscribe.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Glowply offers a single plan that gives you everything you need to build and run a professional website or online store. You can start with a 14-day free trial — no credit card required — and upgrade to a paid subscription whenever you are ready.

## The Glowply plan

The Glowply Plan is currently available at a launch offer price of **699 EGP/month** (originally 1,299 EGP/month). One plan covers everything:

| Feature | Included |
|---|---|
| Website or store built in 45 seconds | Yes |
| Professional templates | Yes |
| Fully Arabic dashboard | Yes |
| Free subdomain (mybusiness.myglowply.com) | Yes |
| Custom domain connection | Yes |
| Integrated online store | Yes |
| Payment gateway integrations | Yes |
| Shipping integrations | Yes |
| Fast technical support via WhatsApp | Yes |
| Secure, fast hosting with SSL | Yes |
| Continuous updates at no extra cost | Yes |

## How the 14-day free trial works

When you create a Glowply account, you automatically start a 14-day free trial. During the trial you have full access to all features — no credit card is required to begin.

<Note>
  When your 14-day free trial ends, your site will be paused until you subscribe to the Glowply Plan. Your data and content are preserved, so you can resume exactly where you left off once you subscribe.
</Note>

## Upgrade from free trial to a paid plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your subscription settings">
    Log in to your Glowply dashboard, click your profile icon in the top-right corner, and select **Subscription** or **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the Glowply Plan">
    Review the plan details and click **Subscribe** or **Upgrade now**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your payment details">
    Provide your card or payment information and confirm the purchase. You will be charged 699 EGP for the first month immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your subscription">
    Once payment is processed, your subscription is active and your site goes live. You will receive a confirmation email with your first invoice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancel your subscription

You can cancel your Glowply subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open subscription settings">
    Go to **Account Settings** → **Subscription** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel your plan">
    Click **Cancel subscription** and follow the on-screen steps to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access until period ends">
    Your site remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your site will be paused.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can cancel at any time from your account settings — no lock-in. If you change your mind, you can resubscribe and your site and content will be restored.
</Tip>

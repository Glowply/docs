---
title: "Update your Glowply account profile and login settings"
description: "Learn how to update your name, email address, phone number, and password in your Glowply account settings. Email changes require email verification."
---

Your Glowply account profile stores your personal contact information and login credentials. You can update any of these details at any time from the **Account** section in your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, except for email address changes, which require verification.

## Update your profile information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Log in to your Glowply dashboard, then click your name or profile icon in the top-right corner and select **Account Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your name">
    In the **Profile** section, update the **First name** and **Last name** fields with your preferred name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your phone number">
    Enter your new phone number in the **Phone** field. This number may be used by the support team to contact you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save changes** to apply your updates. A confirmation message will appear once the changes are saved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change your email address

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Account Settings** from the dashboard menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new email">
    In the **Email** field, replace your current email address with the new one you want to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and verify">
    Click **Save changes**. Glowply will send a verification link to your new email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm via email">
    Open the verification email and click the confirmation link. Your email address will not be updated until you complete this step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Until you confirm the verification link, your old email address remains active. Check your spam or junk folder if you do not receive the verification email within a few minutes.
</Note>

## Change your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Go to **Account Settings** from the dashboard menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the password section">
    Scroll to the **Password** section and click **Change password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current and new password">
    Type your current password, then enter and confirm your new password. Use at least 8 characters for a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your new password">
    Click **Update password**. You will remain logged in on your current device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you have forgotten your current password, log out and use the **Forgot password** link on the login page at glowply.com/my-account/lost-password.
</Tip>

---
title: "View and download your Glowply subscription invoices"
description: "Find out how to access, view, and download your Glowply billing invoices from your account settings. Invoices are issued monthly on your renewal date."
---

Glowply generates an invoice for every billing cycle. You can find and download all your invoices directly from your account settings — no need to contact support. Each invoice includes the amount charged, the billing period, and your subscription details.

## Find and download your invoices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    Log in to your Glowply dashboard, click your profile icon in the top-right corner, and select **Account Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Billing section">
    In the account settings menu, click **Billing** or **Invoices**. You will see a list of all invoices associated with your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the invoice you need">
    Invoices are listed in reverse chronological order, with the most recent at the top. Each row shows the invoice date, billing period, and amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the invoice">
    Click **Download** or the PDF icon next to the invoice you want. The invoice will download as a PDF file to your device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Invoices are issued monthly on your renewal date — the same day of the month that you first subscribed. For example, if you subscribed on the 10th of the month, a new invoice is generated on the 10th of each subsequent month.
</Info>

## What your invoice includes

Each Glowply invoice contains:

- Invoice number and issue date
- Your account name and email address
- Billing period covered
- Plan name and amount charged (in EGP)
- Payment method used

<Note>
  If you need an invoice with specific business details such as a company name or tax registration number, contact the Glowply support team via WhatsApp or by submitting a ticket. See [Contact support](/contact-support) for details.
</Note>

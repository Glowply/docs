Glowply is the fastest way for a small business to get a real website and online store. You sign up, verify your phone, and Glowply automatically builds a complete, hosted site for you at `yourname.myglowply.com` — with secure HTTPS already switched on. No coding, no hosting setup, no designer.

## The two areas you'll use

Glowply has two places you'll work in. Knowing which is which makes everything else easier.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Your account portal" icon="user" href="/en/account/wallet">
    Manage your subscription, top up your **Wallet**, update your details, and open your site editor. This is your billing and account home.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Your site dashboard" icon="store" href="/en/quickstart">
    Build pages with the **Builder**, add products, and run your **Store** — orders, customers, coupons, and more.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

You reach your **site dashboard** from the account portal with one click, so you always start from the same place.

## Who Glowply is for

- Sellers who currently sell on Instagram or WhatsApp and want a real store with a shopping cart
- Small businesses that need a professional website without hiring anyone
- Anyone who wants to be online today, not next month

## How billing works

Glowply uses a **prepaid Wallet**. You top up your Wallet, and your monthly **Subscription** renews automatically from the balance. Every new account starts with a **free trial** — you only need to top up before the trial ends to keep your site running.

<Note>
  Your site address is always `yourname.myglowply.com`. You can connect your own custom domain later through support.
</Note>

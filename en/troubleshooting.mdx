## I didn't receive my verification code

When you sign up, Glowply sends a **6-digit code** to confirm your phone number. It goes to **WhatsApp first**, and falls back to **SMS** if WhatsApp can't reach you. If it hasn't arrived, work through these checks in order.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check WhatsApp, then your SMS inbox">
    Open **WhatsApp** first — the code is sent there before anything else. If it's not in WhatsApp, check your regular **text messages (SMS)**, where it lands as a fallback.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your phone number is correct">
    Glowply accepts **Egyptian phone numbers** only. Make sure you typed your number correctly, with no extra or missing digits — a single wrong digit sends the code somewhere else.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    For the WhatsApp message, your phone needs internet. For the SMS fallback, you need mobile signal. If you have neither, the code can't get through.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend the code">
    Use the **resend** option to request a fresh code. Each code is valid for only a few minutes, so if the first one expired, a new one fixes that.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Still nothing after a minute or two? Resend once more and watch WhatsApp closely — it usually arrives within moments. If your number was entered wrong, go back and correct it, then request the code again.
</Tip>

<Note>
  For the full sign-up and verification walkthrough, see [Sign up & phone verification](/en/account-verification).
</Note>

## I can't log in

You sign in with the **email** and **password** you set when you created your site. If you can't get in, the cause is almost always a forgotten or mistyped password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your email and password carefully">
    Make sure **Caps Lock** is off and there are no extra spaces before or after what you type. Confirm you're using the **same email** you signed up with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    On the login page, choose the **forgot password** link. Enter your account email and submit — Glowply emails you a secure reset link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the reset email">
    Check your inbox for the reset link. If you don't see it within a few minutes, look in your **spam or junk** folder. Open the email and select the link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a new password and sign in">
    Choose a new password (at least **8 characters**), then log in with it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Your **email address can't be changed** from inside your account. If you've lost access to the email on your account, or you're not sure which email you used, [contact support](/en/contact-support) and the team can help you recover access.
</Warning>

<Note>
  For more on signing in and resetting your password, see [Logging in](/en/logging-in).
</Note>

## My site shows past due or is paused

Glowply renews your **Subscription** automatically from your **Wallet** balance. If the balance is too low when a renewal is due, the renewal can't go through — your subscription becomes **past due** and your site may be temporarily paused. The fix is to top up your Wallet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Wallet">
    In your **account portal**, go to **Wallet (المحفظة)**. Look at **needed for renewal** and your **current balance** — the difference is roughly what's missing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top up your Wallet">
    Choose a top-up bundle — **1, 3, 6, or 12 months** — and pay through the secure payment window. For the full steps, see [Wallet & top-ups](/en/account/wallet).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the renewal to retry">
    You don't need to press anything. Once your Wallet has enough credit, Glowply's next automatic retry completes the renewal, your status returns to **active**, and your site comes back.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  To avoid this in future, top up **before** the renewal date, or buy a longer bundle so several renewals are covered at once. Your **Wallet** and your portal **Dashboard** both show the next renewal date and warn you in advance.
</Tip>

<Note>
  For exactly how renewals and recovery work, see [Renewals & past due](/en/account/renewals-and-past-due).
</Note>

## I paid but my Wallet didn't update

After a successful top-up, your balance updates **once the payment is confirmed**. A short delay is normal — here's how to tell the difference between "still confirming" and a payment that didn't go through.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Give it a moment to confirm">
    A brief **pending** state right after paying is normal. Wait a short while and refresh the **Wallet** page — the credit appears as soon as the payment is confirmed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your transaction history">
    On the Wallet page, look at **Transaction history**. A successful top-up shows as a **Wallet top-up (＋)** row with the amount and date. If you see it there, your balance has been credited.
  </Step>
  <Step title="If the payment was declined, try again">
    If the payment was **declined or cancelled**, nothing is charged and no credit is added. Start the top-up again and complete payment. Card payments use **3-D Secure**, so finish any verification step your bank shows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  A declined or abandoned payment never deducts money — your balance simply stays the same. If you're certain a payment left your bank but your Wallet still hasn't updated after a while, [contact support](/en/contact-support) with the date and amount and we'll check it.
</Warning>

<Note>
  For how the payment window works, see [Payments](/en/account/payments). For reading your balance and history, see [Wallet & top-ups](/en/account/wallet).
</Note>

## More common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The site address I want is taken">
    Each address can belong to only one site. If the name you want isn't available, try a small variation — add your city, your product, or a number, like `nour-store` or `cairo-flowers`. Remember the rules: **lowercase English letters, numbers, and hyphens** only; **3 to 63 characters**; and no hyphen at the start or end.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want to use my own custom domain">
    You can keep your `yourname.myglowply.com` address and map your own **custom domain** on top of it. Custom domains are set up for you rather than self-serve — [contact support](/en/contact-support) to request one. See [Site management](/en/account/site-management) for what to expect.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I need to change my email address">
    Your email is **read-only** inside your account — it's the address you log in with and where receipts are sent. To change it, [contact support](/en/contact-support) and the team will update it for you. See [Account settings](/en/account/account-settings).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want to cancel or change my plan">
    Cancelling, upgrading, or switching plans isn't done from the portal. [Contact support](/en/contact-support) and we'll help. Note that simply not topping up will leave an unpaid renewal **past due** rather than cancelling your subscription — see [Renewals & past due](/en/account/renewals-and-past-due).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My site isn't loading">
    First check that your subscription isn't **past due** — open your portal **Dashboard** for a red alert, and top up your **Wallet** if needed (see above). Your site's address is always `yourname.myglowply.com`, and its security certificate (HTTPS) is handled automatically, so there's nothing to configure on your side. If your subscription is active and the site still won't load after a little while, [contact support](/en/contact-support) with your site address.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't find the button to open my site editor">
    You open your **site dashboard** from the **account portal**. Look for the **Glowply dashboard** button on your portal Dashboard or in **Site management** — it opens your editor and Store securely in a new tab. If a pop-up blocker stops the new tab, allow pop-ups for Glowply and try again.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

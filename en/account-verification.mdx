Signing up for Glowply is a single short form followed by a quick phone check. You enter your details, confirm your phone number with a **6-digit code**, and Glowply builds your site and drops you into your **account portal**. This page walks through every field and what to expect from the verification step.

<Note>
  Want the fast version? The [Create your site](/en/quickstart) quickstart covers sign-up to a live site in one short flow. This page is the detailed reference for the form fields and the verification code.
</Note>

## Open the sign-up form

Go to [glowply.com](https://glowply.com) and choose **Start now (ابدأ الآن)**. This opens the sign-up form, where you'll complete every field below in one place.

## Fill in the form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your site address">
    This is the web address your site will live at: `yourname.myglowply.com`. Type the **yourname** part, and Glowply checks whether it's available as you type.

    Your site address must:

    - Use only **lowercase English letters, numbers, and hyphens** (`-`)
    - Be **3 to 63 characters** long
    - **Not start or end** with a hyphen

    Examples that work: `cairo-flowers`, `nour-store`, `bestshop2025`.

    <Tip>
      Pick something short and easy to say out loud — you'll share it with customers. You can map your own custom domain later; see [Site management](/en/account/site-management).
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your name">
    Add your **first name** and **last name**. These appear on your account and in support conversations. You can edit them later from your [Account settings](/en/account/account-settings).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Add the **email address** for your account. You'll use it to sign in, and Glowply sends receipts and any password-reset links here.

    <Note>
      Each email can be used for one account. After sign-up, your email is read-only — to change it, contact support. See [Account settings](/en/account/account-settings).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your phone number">
    Add your **Egyptian phone number**. Glowply sends your verification code here in the next step, so use a number you can check right now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a password">
    Choose a **password** of at least **8 characters**. A longer mix of letters, numbers, and symbols is harder to guess.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Submit the form to continue to phone verification. If anything needs fixing — an address that's taken or a field left blank — Glowply points it out so you can correct it before moving on.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Two limits to know before you sign up:

  - **Egyptian phone numbers only.** Sign-up accepts Egyptian numbers.
  - **One free trial per phone number.** Each phone number can start one trial. Your phone number and email must each be unique — they can't already be in use on another account.
</Warning>

## Verify your phone

To prove the number is yours, Glowply sends a one-time code that you type back into the form.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Wait for the code">
    Glowply sends a **6-digit code** to your phone — on **WhatsApp first**, with an **SMS** fallback if WhatsApp doesn't reach you. It usually arrives within moments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    Type the 6 digits into the verification field to confirm your number.

    <Note>The code is valid for only a few minutes. Enter it soon after it arrives.</Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend if needed">
    Didn't get it? You can **resend** the code. Check that your phone number was typed correctly and that you have signal or an internet connection for WhatsApp.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  No code after a minute or two? Open WhatsApp to confirm the message didn't land there, then use **resend**. If it's still missing, your SMS inbox is the fallback.
</Tip>

## After you verify

Once your code is accepted, your account is created and Glowply builds your site automatically — environment, template, domain, SSL certificate, and Store. This usually finishes in about **20 seconds**, and a **free trial** subscription starts for you.

When the build is done, you land in your **account portal**, never a technical screen. From there you can open your **site dashboard** to start editing.

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The code never arrived. What do I do?">
    First, **resend** the code. Confirm your phone number was entered correctly and that your phone has signal or internet. The code comes on **WhatsApp first**; if WhatsApp can't deliver, it falls back to **SMS**. Each code is valid for only a few minutes, so request a fresh one if the old one expired.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My site address is taken.">
    Each address can belong to only one site. If `yourname` is unavailable, try a small variation — add your city, your product, or a number, like `nour-store` or `cairo-flowers`. Remember the rules: lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only; 3 to 63 characters; no leading or trailing hyphen.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I sign up with a phone number from outside Egypt?">
    Sign-up accepts **Egyptian phone numbers**. Use an Egyptian number you can check, since that's where your verification code is sent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="It says my phone or email is already in use.">
    Each phone number and each email can be tied to only one account, and each phone number gets **one free trial**. If you already have an account, [sign in](/en/logging-in) instead. If you think this is a mistake, [contact support](/en/contact-support).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to sign up?">
    No. Every new account starts with a **free trial**, so no payment is needed to create your site. You only top up your **Wallet** before the trial ends to keep your site running after that. See [Wallet & top-ups](/en/account/wallet).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my site address later?">
    Your `yourname.myglowply.com` address stays with your account. If you'd like to use your own custom domain, you can request that through support — see [Site management](/en/account/site-management).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Already verified and just need to get back in? Head to [Logging in](/en/logging-in).
</Note>

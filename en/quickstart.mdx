Creating your Glowply site takes only a few minutes. You fill in a short form, verify your phone with a code, and Glowply builds and publishes your site automatically.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sign-up page">
    Go to [glowply.com](https://glowply.com) and choose **Start now (ابدأ الآن)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your site address">
    Pick the address your site will live at: `yourname.myglowply.com`.

    Your name must:
    - Use only **lowercase English letters, numbers, and hyphens** (`-`)
    - Be **3–63 characters** long
    - Not start or end with a hyphen

    Glowply checks availability as you type. Examples: `cairo-flowers`, `nour-store`, `bestshop2025`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Enter your first name, last name, email, phone number, and a password (at least 8 characters).

    <Note>Use an Egyptian phone number — it's how Glowply sends your verification code and reaches you for support.</Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your phone">
    Glowply sends a **6-digit code** to your phone — on **WhatsApp first**, with an **SMS** fallback. Enter it to confirm your number. The code is valid for a few minutes; you can resend it if it doesn't arrive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch your site build">
    Glowply now sets everything up automatically — your environment, template, domain, SSL certificate, and store. This usually finishes in about 20 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're live">
    Your site is published at `yourname.myglowply.com` and you land in your **account portal**. From here, open **Glowply dashboard** to start editing your site and adding products.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

<Card title="Top up your Wallet" icon="wallet" href="/en/account/wallet">
  Add credit so your subscription keeps renewing after the free trial ends.
</Card>

From your **site dashboard** you can then edit pages with the Builder, add your first products to the Store, and set up your blog.

<Note>
  Every new account starts with a **free trial** — no credit card needed to sign up. Just top up your Wallet before the trial ends to keep your site running.
</Note>

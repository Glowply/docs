Your **site dashboard** is the control center for your website and online store. It's your own site's admin area, in Arabic, where you design pages, add products, take orders, and grow your business. This is different from your **account portal**, which is only for billing — your subscription, **Wallet**, and account details.

<Info>
  **Two areas, one rule of thumb:** use your **account portal (لوحة حسابك)** for billing and your **site dashboard** for building the site and running the store. When in doubt, ask "am I paying for Glowply, or am I working on my site?"
</Info>

## Open your site dashboard

Your site dashboard opens from your account portal — you don't sign in separately.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your account portal">
    Sign in to Glowply and open your **account portal**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Glowply dashboard">
    On your site card, choose **Glowply dashboard**. Glowply signs you in securely and opens your dashboard in a new browser tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start working on your site">
    You land on the dashboard home, with everything you need to set up and manage your site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Keep both tabs open while you work: your **account portal** in one, your **site dashboard** in the other. Switch between them whenever you need to.
</Tip>

## The dashboard home

The first screen you see is your dashboard home. It welcomes you and gives you a quick read on how your site and store are doing.

### Quick tour to set up your store

New sites show a **setup checklist** — a quick tour, around seven short steps, that walks you through getting your store ready to sell. It covers the essentials, such as adding your details, your first products, and your store settings. Work through it from top to bottom.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the checklist">
    On the dashboard home, find the setup checklist near the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete one step at a time">
    Click a step to open the page where you finish that task, then return to the checklist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch your progress fill in">
    Each finished step is marked complete, so you always know what's left.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The checklist is a guide, not a lock. You can explore the dashboard freely and come back to any step later.
</Note>

### Your store at a glance

Below the welcome, the dashboard home shows live numbers and highlights so you can see how your store is performing without opening any reports:

- **Store stats** — key totals for your store, including **revenue**, **orders**, **items sold**, and **average order value**.
- **Live visitors** — how many people are on your site right now.
- **Best-selling products** — the items customers are buying most.
- **New orders** — your most recent orders, so you can act on them quickly.

<Tip>
  For deeper reports — sales trends over time, or where your visitors come from — open the **Marketing** section, which includes **Store analytics** and **Site analytics**.
</Tip>

## The left-side menu

A menu on the side of the dashboard groups everything into sections. Here's what each one is for:

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard (لوحة التحكم)** | The home screen — your setup checklist, store stats, and recent activity. |
| **Store (المتجر)** | Run your online store: products, orders, customers, categories, tags, attributes, reviews, coupons, and form submissions. |
| **Pages (الصفحات)** | Your website's pages, such as Home, About, and Contact — edited with the **Builder**. |
| **Media (مكتبة الوسائط)** | Your library of images and files to use across the site. |
| **Posts (المقالات)** | Your blog — articles with categories, tags, and comments. |
| **Marketing (التسويق)** | Grow your business: SEO, site and store analytics, customer journeys, and live events. |
| **Builder (البلدر)** | The visual page editor and its design tools — where you build and style your pages. |

<Note>
  Your pages are designed in the **Builder (البلدر)** — Glowply's drag-and-drop visual editor. You don't need to write any code.
</Note>

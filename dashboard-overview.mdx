After you log in to Glowply, you land on your dashboard. This is the central control panel for everything related to your website and online store. The entire interface is in Arabic, so every label, menu, and setting is displayed in your language.

The dashboard is divided into clearly labeled sections along the sidebar. Each section handles a different part of your site.

## Dashboard sections

### Site editor

The site editor is where you visually customize your website. Using a drag-and-drop interface, you can:

- Change your site's color scheme and fonts
- Upload and replace images
- Edit text, headings, and sections
- Rearrange and add page blocks

No code is involved. Every change you make is applied live. See the [website editor guide](/website/editor) for a full walkthrough.

### Pages management

The pages section lets you manage all the pages that make up your website. You can add new pages, reorder them in the navigation menu, show or hide them from visitors, and set each page's title. See [managing pages](/website/pages) for details.

### Products and store management

The store section is where you build your product catalog. From here you can:

- Add, edit, and remove products
- Set prices, descriptions, and images
- Organize products into categories
- Control product visibility

Visit [store products](/store/products) to get started adding your first items.

### Orders

The orders section shows every purchase placed through your store. You can view order details, update order status, and track fulfillment from a single list. Learn more in the [orders guide](/store/orders).

### Payments and shipping settings

Configure how customers pay and how you deliver:

- **Payments** — connect supported payment gateways so customers can check out. See [payments setup](/store/payments).
- **Shipping** — define shipping zones, rates, and delivery options. See [shipping settings](/store/shipping).

### Domain settings

By default, your site lives at `yourname.myglowply.com`. On a paid plan, you can connect a custom domain (e.g. `yourbusiness.com`) so your brand has its own address. The domain settings section guides you through the connection process. See [custom domain setup](/website/domain).

### Account and billing

Manage your personal details, subscription plan, and invoices from the account section:

- Update your name, email, and phone number
- View your current plan and renewal date
- Upgrade from the free trial to a paid plan
- Download past invoices

See [subscription and billing](/account/subscription) for more information.

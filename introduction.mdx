Glowply is an AI-powered website and e-commerce builder made for Arabic-speaking small business owners, freelancers, and online sellers. You answer a few simple questions, and Glowply's AI generates a complete, live website for you in under 45 seconds — no coding, no design experience, and no hosting setup needed.

## Who is Glowply for?

Glowply is built for anyone who needs a professional online presence without the complexity of traditional web tools:

- **Small business owners** who want a website and store that works from day one
- **Freelancers** who need a portfolio or service page to share with clients
- **E-commerce sellers** who want to sell products online with a built-in shopping cart and payment processing
- **Anyone** who has tried other website builders and found them too technical or too expensive

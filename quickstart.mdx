Creating a Glowply website takes less than a minute. You fill in a short registration form, and Glowply's AI automatically builds your site, sets up your hosting, and activates your SSL certificate — all before you've had a chance to make a cup of tea.

<Note>
  Glowply includes a 14-day free trial with full access to every feature. No credit card is required to sign up.
</Note>

## Create your site

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the registration page">
    Open [glowply.com/start-now](https://glowply.com/start-now) in your browser. This is the only page you need to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your site address">
    Enter the subdomain you want for your site. Your address will follow the format `mybusiness.myglowply.com`.

    Follow these rules when choosing your subdomain:

    - Use only **lowercase letters**, **numbers**, and **hyphens** ( `-` )
    - Between **3 and 63 characters** long
    - Cannot start or end with a hyphen

    For example: `cairo-flowers`, `ahmed-designs`, or `bestshop2025` are all valid subdomains.

    <Tip>
      Choose a subdomain that matches your business name. You can connect a custom domain (e.g. `mybusiness.com`) later from your dashboard on a paid plan.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Complete the remaining registration fields:

    - **First name** and **last name**
    - **Email address** — used to log in and receive notifications
    - **Password** — choose something secure and memorable
    - **Phone number** — used for account recovery and WhatsApp support
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Register">
    Click the **Register** button. Glowply's AI immediately begins building your site. Within seconds, your fully-hosted website is live at your chosen address — no further action needed from you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the editor and start customizing">
    Once your site is ready, you are taken directly to the dashboard. From there, open the **Site Editor** to customize your colors, images, text, and layout using the drag-and-drop interface.

    See the [dashboard overview](/dashboard-overview) to learn what each section of the dashboard does, or jump straight to the [website editor guide](/website/editor) to start making changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next?

After registration, your site is already live and accessible. Here are the most common next steps:

- **Add your products** — go to [Store → Products](/store/products) to list items for sale
- **Customize your design** — use the [website editor](/website/editor) to match your brand
- **Set up payments and shipping** — configure how you get paid and how you deliver orders
- **Connect a custom domain** — replace `.myglowply.com` with your own domain on a paid plan

<Info>
  Your site includes free SSL, managed hosting, and automatic updates from the moment it is created. You do not need to configure any of this yourself.
</Info>

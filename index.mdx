## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [glowply.com/start-now](https://glowply.com/start-now), choose your site address (e.g. `mybusiness.myglowply.com`), and fill in your details. No credit card required for the 14-day free trial.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Glowply builds your site">
    Glowply's AI picks a professional template that fits your business type, sets up your domain, and provisions a fully-hosted environment — all in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize with drag and drop">
    Open the visual editor to update your colors, images, text, and products. Every change is live immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live and start selling">
    Your site is online from the first moment. Share your link, connect a custom domain, and start receiving customers or orders the same day.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Glowply includes free hosting, SSL, and automatic updates on every plan. You own your content — no lock-in.
</Note>

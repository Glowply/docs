---
title: "Add, reorder, and manage your Glowply website pages"
description: "Learn how to add new pages, edit existing ones, reorder them in your site navigation, set your homepage, and delete pages you no longer need."
---

Your Glowply site can have as many pages as you need. You manage all of them from the **Pages** section of your dashboard, where you can add, rename, reorder, and remove pages at any time.

<Note>
  Most business sites include a core set of pages: **Home**, **About**, **Services**, **Contact**, and optionally a **Blog**, **Gallery**, or **Pricing** page. You can start with these and add more as your business grows.
</Note>

## Common tasks

### Add a new page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pages">
    From the dashboard, click **Pages** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Page">
    Click the **+ Add Page** button at the top of the page list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the page details">
    Give the page a name (for example, "Services") and choose a starting template or a blank layout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start editing">
    Click **Create Page**. The new page opens in the visual editor. Add your content using the drag-and-drop editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Edit an existing page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pages">
    Click **Pages** in the dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the page">
    Click the page name or click the pencil icon next to it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit in the visual editor">
    The page opens in the editor. Make your changes — they are saved and live immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reorder pages in the navigation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pages">
    Click **Pages** in the dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag to reorder">
    Hover over a page row to reveal the drag handle on the left. Drag the page up or down to change its position in your site's navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the order">
    Release the page in the new position. The navigation menu on your live site updates immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Set the homepage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pages">
    Click **Pages** in the dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open page settings">
    Click the three-dot menu next to the page you want to make the homepage, then click **Set as Homepage**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Delete a page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pages">
    Click **Pages** in the dashboard sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open page settings">
    Click the three-dot menu next to the page, then click **Delete**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    A confirmation dialog appears. Click **Delete Page** to permanently remove the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a page is permanent. All content on that page will be removed and cannot be recovered. Make sure you no longer need the page before deleting it.
</Warning>

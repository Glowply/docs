---
title: "Customize your Glowply site branding, colors, and fonts"
description: "Learn how to update your logo, color palette, typography, and button styles to match your brand consistently across your entire Glowply website."
---

Glowply gives you full control over your site's visual identity. Every site starts from a professional template matched to your business type — you can then adjust the colors, fonts, logo, and button styles to reflect your brand without touching any code.

<Tip>
  Keep your brand consistent by choosing one or two primary colors and one font pairing. A cohesive visual identity builds trust with visitors and makes your site look polished.
</Tip>

## Accessing design settings

From the dashboard, click **Design** (or **Customize**) in the left sidebar. The design panel opens alongside a live preview of your site so you can see every change as you make it.

## Customization areas

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Logo">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Logo section">
        In the design panel, click **Logo**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload your logo">
        Click **Upload Logo** and select an image file from your device. PNG or SVG files with a transparent background work best.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Adjust the size">
        Use the size slider to set how large the logo appears in the navigation bar. Click **Save** when done.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Colors">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Colors section">
        In the design panel, click **Colors**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose your primary color">
        Click the color swatch next to **Primary Color** and pick a color using the color picker, or type a hex code directly. This color is used for buttons, links, and key accents throughout the site.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set a secondary color">
        Repeat the process for **Secondary Color**, which is applied to backgrounds, section highlights, and hover states.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Preview and save">
        The live preview updates as you pick colors. Click **Save** to apply the palette to your entire site.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Fonts">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Fonts section">
        In the design panel, click **Fonts**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a heading font">
        Click the **Heading Font** dropdown and browse the available typefaces. Click any font to preview it on your site.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a body font">
        Repeat for **Body Font**, which applies to paragraphs and general text across the site.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save your selection">
        Click **Save**. The new fonts are applied sitewide immediately.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Buttons">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Buttons section">
        In the design panel, click **Buttons**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select a button style">
        Choose from rounded, square, or pill-shaped buttons. You can also adjust the border width and shadow.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm the changes">
        The preview shows how buttons will look across your pages. Click **Save** to apply.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Starting from a different template

If you want a completely different look, you can switch your site's base template at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Templates">
    In the design panel, click **Templates**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and preview">
    Scroll through the available templates. Click any template to preview it with your existing content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply the template">
    Click **Apply Template**. Your content stays in place — only the layout and styling change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Applying a new template rearranges your page layout. Review each page in the editor after switching to make sure everything looks right.
</Warning>

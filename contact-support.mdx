## Before you contact support

The faster you provide context, the faster the team can help you. When reaching out via WhatsApp or submitting a ticket, include:

- Your **site URL** (e.g., `mybusiness.myglowply.com` or your custom domain)
- A clear **description of the issue**, including what you were trying to do and what happened instead
- Any **error messages** you see — a screenshot is helpful
- The **browser and device** you are using, if the issue is visual

<Tip>
  Including your site URL and a description of the issue when you first contact support helps the team resolve your case without needing to ask follow-up questions.
</Tip>
